# AI Study Buddy 💻✨

**Personalized AI Learning Platform for Smart Studying**  

Studying can be overwhelming with scattered PDFs, lecture notes, and references. **AI Study Buddy** helps students organize their materials, generate concise summaries, create flashcards, quizzes, and even personalized study plans — all in one platform!  

---

## 🌟 Features

- **Upload PDFs & Notes** 📂  
  Easily upload your study material (PDFs or text notes).

- **Topic-wise Summaries** 🧠  
  AI parses your files and generates concise, organized summaries.

- **Flashcards** 🃏  
  Quick memory recall with interactive question-answer flashcards.

- **Quizzes** 📝  
  Self-assessment with AI-generated multiple-choice questions.

- **Personalized Study Plan** 🎯  
  AI creates day-wise study plans based on your timeline and exam goals.

- **Chat with Notes** 💬  
  Ask questions and clarify concepts directly from your uploaded materials.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** React (Vite) + Tailwind CSS / Pico.css  
- **Backend:** FastAPI (Python)  
- **AI API:** Google Gemini / OpenAI GPT  
- **PDF Parsing:** PyPDF2 / pdfplumber
